About Aeronourish Technologies

Bridging Traditional Wisdom with Modern Innovation

Aeronourish Technologies is a student-led agritech startup initiative incubated at Dr. Mahalingam College of Engineering and Technology (MCET), Pollachi, Tamil Nadu. Founded by passionate engineers from agriculture-rooted families, we're committed to redefining farming practices through practical, sustainable, and technology-driven solutions.

Our work focuses on developing hybrid farming systems that integrate aeroponics and hydroponics, supported by AI-powered automation, IoT-based monitoring, and real-time mobile control.
